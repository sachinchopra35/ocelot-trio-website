About Ocelot Trio

The Ocelot Trio brings together three passionate musicians united by their love for modern jazz. Our sound blends traditional jazz foundations with contemporary influences, creating a unique musical experience that captivates audiences.

Our Trio was born in London in 2024, and has since been coming up with original tunes that reflect the distinct styles of each member. We hope our unique sound will makes waves in the UK Jazz scene and beyond.

Sachin (Piano)

Combining innovative techniques to combine the best of jazz, funk, neo-soul, hip-hop, and even classical

Dylan (Bass)

An expert at driving the rhythms of complex songs, while singing out deep melodies that intertwine with the piano and drums

Max (Drums)

An unbelievably versatile drummer, able to seamlessly fuse Jazz and Neo-Soul with heavier styles like Drum and Bass and Hip-Hop
